Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

1. Introduction & Scope

At Daug’s Desserts, your trust matters. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your information when you interact with our website, order forms, Shopify checkout, Google-powered tools, and delivery services.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide Directly

  • Name
  • Email
  • Phone number
  • Shipping address
  • Gift recipient details
  • Order preferences & product selections
  • Custom notes (To/From/Message)
  • Allergy notes or special instructions

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address
  • Device & browser info
  • Site traffic & usage data
  • Cookie tracking data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfill cookie orders
  • Create accurate shipping labels
  • Send order confirmations & updates
  • Improve our menu, packaging, and services
  • Support customer service needs
  • Prevent fraud and incorrect orders

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

We only share data with trusted partners such as Google tools, Stripe for payments, and shipping carriers.

5. User Choices & Control

  • Update your info anytime
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Request a copy of your stored info
  • Opt out of communication

6. Cookies

You may disable cookies via your browser. Some features may be impacted.

7. Children’s Privacy

Daug’s Desserts does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Data Security

We use Google’s encrypted storage, secure Stripe processing, and limited data access.

9. Shipping & Delivery Data

We share your address only with shipping carriers. Delivery data may be stored temporarily for tracking.

10. International Users

Data is stored and processed in the United States.

11. Policy Updates

We may update this policy and will post changes on our website.
