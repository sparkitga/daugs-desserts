Our Story

Daug’s Desserts was created by Augie “Daug” Oberkrum and his mother, Diane — a duo who brought decades of catering experience, creativity, and warmth into every recipe. What began as a family passion turned into a way to share joy, comfort, and connection one cookie at a time.

Every Daug’s Desserts cookie is made with intention — crafted to brighten a day, spark a memory, or help you slow down for a sweet moment with the people you love. Whether you're gifting a box or enjoying your own, we’re here to help moments feel a little more meaningful.

If a cookie has made your day or your story includes us in some small way, we’d love to hear it. Send us a message anytime. Your smiles inspire everything we bake.
