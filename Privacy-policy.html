Daug's Desserts' Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025 1. Introduction & Scope At Daug’s Desserts, your trust matters. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your information when you interact with our: • Website • Order forms • Custom note forms • Google-powered tools (Google Forms, Google Sheets, Google Analytics, Gmail, etc.) • Digital communication • Shipping and delivery services This policy does not apply to outside platforms we link to (e.g., Instagram, Facebook, Stripe). 2. Information We Collect We collect information in two ways: (A) information you share directly and (B) information collected automatically. A. Information You Provide Directly When you order baked goods, submit a custom note, or use any Daug’s Desserts form, you may provide: Contact & Shipping Information • Name (First & Last) • Email address • Phone number • Delivery or shipping address • Recipient name & address (for gifts) Order & Product Information • Product selections (cookie boxes, single cookies, custom packs) • Quantity • Special instructions • Custom messages (“To:” “From:” “Note:” fields) • Preferred delivery date • Allergy concerns or dietary notes Customer Interests • Favorite cookies or flavors • Gift options • Repeat-order preferences We use this information to prepare, package, and deliver your baked goods safely and accurately. Data from your forms is securely stored through Google Sheets and Google Forms, and email confirmations are sent through Gmail. B. Information Collected Automatically When you visit our site, Google-powered tools may collect: • IP address • Browser type • Device type • Site usage and traffic data • Interaction with product pages • Cookie performance data We may use: • Google Analytics (basic traffic insights) • Google Tag Manager • Standard browser cookies This helps us improve the ordering experience and website performance. 3. How We Use Your Information We use your information to: • Process and fulfill cookie orders • Prepare accurate delivery and shipping labels • Communicate order status, confirmations, and questions • Provide customer support • Personalize your gifting or order experience • Improve our menu, packaging, and customer service • Prevent fraud or duplicated orders • Maintain a record of past orders for quality assurance We may also use your contact information to clarify delivery issues (e.g., address errors, time windows). If you submit a custom note, your message is securely stored through Google Sheets and printed for inclusion in your order. 4. Information Sharing & Disclosure We do not sell personal information. We may share your information only with trusted partners who help us operate Daug’s Desserts, such as: • Google (Forms, Sheets, Analytics, Gmail) • Stripe, for secure payment processing • Shipping carriers (USPS, UPS, FedEx, local couriers) • Delivery partners, when applicable These partners follow their own privacy and security standards. 5. User Choices & Control You may: • Request to update your information • Correct incorrect contact or shipping details • Ask us to delete your information after your order is complete • Opt out of any promotional communication • Request a copy of the information stored in our Google Sheets You can exercise your rights by emailing: daugsdessertsmarketing@gmail.com If you submitted incorrect information, let us know immediately so we can make corrections before baking or shipping. 6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies Daug’s Desserts uses basic cookies and Google tracking tools to improve site experience and ordering performance. You may disable cookies in your browser. Some features (analytics, loading speed) may be impacted but you will still be able to order. 7. Children’s Privacy Our bakery and website are not targeted to children under 13. We do not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13 without verified parental consent. If you believe a child submitted information by mistake, contact us right away at: daugsdessertsmarketing@gmail.com 8. Data Security We take your privacy seriously and work hard to protect your information. Our safeguards include: • Secure Google cloud storage • Google Forms and Sheets’ built-in encryption • Secure payment processing through Stripe • Limited access to customer forms • No physical printing of data except shipping labels or customer notes While no digital system is 100% perfect, we are committed to handling your information with care and integrity. 9. Shipping, Delivery & Address Data By placing an order, you agree that: • Your address may be shared with shipping carriers • We may contact you or the recipient for delivery issues • Delivery data is stored temporarily for tracking and service quality • Address information may be kept for repeat customer support or troubleshooting (unless deletion is requested) We do not use address information for marketing unless voluntarily provided. 10. International Users Daug’s Desserts operates in the United States. By purchasing or interacting with our website, international users acknowledge that their data will be processed and stored in the U.S. 11. Policy Updates We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in: • Google tools • Our menu • Our shipping and delivery process • Legal requirements • New online ordering features If major changes occur, we will notify customers on our website or by email. 12. Contact Information If you have any questions about your data or privacy, contact: Daug’s Desserts Privacy Team daugsdessertsmarketing@gmail.com Canton, Georgia We will respond within 48 hours.
