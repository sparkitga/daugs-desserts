Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

1. Introduction & Scope

At Daug’s Desserts, your trust matters. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your information when you interact with our website, order forms, Google tools, digital communications, and shipping services. This policy does not apply to outside platforms (Instagram, Facebook, Stripe, etc.).

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide Directly

When placing an order or submitting a custom note, you may provide:

  • Name
  • Email
  • Phone number
  • Shipping or delivery address
  • Recipient details for gifts
  • Custom messages
  • Order selections & quantity
  • Allergy notes or preferences

This data is stored in secure Google Sheets and Google Forms, and confirmations are sent through Gmail.

B. Information Collected Automatically

Google-powered tools may collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser & device type
  • Site usage data
  • Cookie performance data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfill orders
  • Prepare accurate shipping labels
  • Communicate updates or delivery questions
  • Personalize your ordering or gifting experience
  • Improve menu and service offerings
  • Prevent fraud or duplicate orders
  • Store notes for gifting or customer support

4. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We only share data with trusted partners including:

  • Google (Forms, Sheets, Analytics, Gmail)
  • Stripe payment processing
  • USPS, UPS, FedEx, or local couriers

5. User Choices & Control

You may request to:

  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your data after order completion
  • Opt out of promotional communication
  • Request a copy of stored data

Email: daugsdessertsmarketing@gmail.com

6. Cookies & Tracking

Daug’s Desserts uses basic cookies and Google Analytics tools to improve website speed and experience. You may disable cookies, though some features may be affected.

7. Children’s Privacy

We do not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe information was submitted by a child, please contact us immediately.

8. Data Security

We protect your data using Google’s encrypted systems, Stripe’s secure payment platform, and limited-access storage within our bakery operations.

9. Shipping, Delivery & Address Data

Your address may be shared with shipping carriers, and delivery data may be temporarily stored to ensure order accuracy and support future troubleshooting.

10. International Users

Daug’s Desserts operates in the United States. By ordering, international customers agree that their data will be stored in the U.S.

11. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Significant changes will be noted on our website.

12. Contact Us

Email: daugsdessertsmarketing@gmail.com

Canton, Georgia

We typically respond within 48 hours.
